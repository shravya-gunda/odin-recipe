Omlette Recipe

Description

a dish made from eggs that are mixed together, cooked without stirring, and served folded in half often with a filling of cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In small bowl, crack the eggs and whisk them with a fork
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Melt the butter in a non stick pan over mdeium heat. Coat the pan with the butter, then pour the eggs.
  4. Cook the omlette for 30 seconds. Then using a spatula flip the eggs and cook on the other side for more 30 seconds.
  5. Flip the omelette onto a plate. Serve immediately
